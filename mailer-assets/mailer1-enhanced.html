🥾 Hidden Gem: Costa's Walk Trail

Breathtaking views from Costa's Walk trail

The hidden mountain spring - a perfect spot to refresh

If you haven't experienced Costa's Walk yet, you're missing one of our estate's best-kept secrets. This scenic trail has become a must-do for guests who want to truly connect with the history and beauty of the Spekboom Valley.

Why guests love this trail:

  • Ancient Bokoni Ruins: Walk through historical stone structures from the 1500s-1800s
  • Mountain Spring: Discover a hidden treasure with crystal-clear water
  • Panoramic Views: Sweeping vistas of the Spekboom Valley and surrounding mountains
  • Picnic Spot: Perfect shaded area to relax and enjoy the scenery
  • Easy Access: Well-cleared trail suitable for most fitness levels

Pro Tip:

The best time to walk Costa's Trail is early morning or late afternoon when the light is perfect for photography and the temperatures are comfortable.

🛣️ Getting Here: What You Need to Know

If you haven't visited recently, you'll be pleasantly surprised by the journey to Rivendell. Here's the latest on road conditions and what to expect.

Great News: The Main Road is Fixed!

The Belfast–Dullstroom–Lydenburg road has been completely resurfaced. What used to be a bumpy, pothole-filled journey is now smooth sailing all the way.

Your journey will be:

  • Much more comfortable and stress-free
  • Faster with no need to dodge potholes
  • Easier on your vehicle
  • More enjoyable with the scenic views

We've driven it ourselves and can confirm it's a world of difference!

Vehicle Note:

The main road is excellent, but there's still 11km of gravel access road. We recommend SUVs, bakkies, or vehicles with higher clearance than sports sedans for the best experience.
